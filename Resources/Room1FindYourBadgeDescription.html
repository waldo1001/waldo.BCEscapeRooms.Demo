Room 1: Find Your Badge

TL;DR

Your conference badge has the wrong company name on it. Fix it in Business Central so the registration desk can reprint it. The system checks automatically when you click Update Status.

The Challenge

You just arrived at the Directions conference, but the badge they printed shows your old company name. The registration desk won't let you in until your badge matches the conference records. Every minute you spend here is a session you're missing.

Your Mission

Fix Your Badge

Somewhere in Business Central, there is a page where your company details are stored. The conference records expect the company name to read Directions 2026.

  1. Find the page in Business Central where company details are maintained
  2. Update the company name to match what the conference expects

Hint: Search for something related to your company's own information.

Click Update Status on the room page after making the change. The system will verify the name is correct.

Update Status: Not all steps are captured automatically. Hit the Update Status button on the room page to check if you have completed steps that weren't registered yet.
