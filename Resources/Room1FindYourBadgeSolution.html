Solution: Room 1 - Find Your Badge

The company name is stored on the Company Information page. You need to change it to match the conference records.

Fix Your Badge

Step 1: Open Company Information

  1. In Business Central, use the search bar (magnifying glass or Alt+Q)
  2. Search for Company Information
  3. Open the Company Information page

Step 2: Update the Company Name

  1. Find the Name field at the top of the page
  2. Change it to Directions 2026 (exact spelling matters)
  3. Close the page (the change saves automatically)

Why This Matters:

  • The task uses polling validation — the framework calls IsValid() when you click Update Status
  • IsValid() reads the Company Information record and checks the Name field
  • No event subscriber is needed because we're checking an existing record's field value

Verification

  • Company Information page shows Name = Directions 2026
  • Task status shows Completed after clicking Update Status
  • Room 1 closes and Room 2 opens automatically

Important: Don't forget to click the Update Status button.

What You've Accomplished

  • Found and modified company-level data in Business Central
  • Experienced the polling validation pattern — the simplest form of task validation
