Room 2: Network or Perish

TL;DR

Create a new contact to represent a partner you met at the conference. The system detects the moment you create the right contact and completes the task automatically.

The Challenge

The Directions conference is all about making connections with fellow partners. You've got your badge, you're in the venue, but the organizers track networking activity. You need to log at least one new connection before you can leave. First impressions count — make sure the contact represents a Directions Partner.

Your Mission

Make a Connection

Find where Business Central stores contact information and create a new contact that represents a partner you met at the conference. The company name on the contact is what the organizers check.

  1. Navigate to the contact list in Business Central
  2. Create a new contact record
  3. Set the company name to Directions Partner

Hint: Search for contacts. The company name field is what matters, not the contact's personal name.

This task completes automatically the moment you create the right contact. No need to click Update Status — but you can if you want to double-check.

Update Status: Not all steps are captured automatically. Hit the Update Status button on the room page to check if you have completed steps that weren't registered yet.
