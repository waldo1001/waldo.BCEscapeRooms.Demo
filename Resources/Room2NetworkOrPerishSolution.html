Solution: Room 2 - Network or Perish

You need to create a Contact record with Company Name set to "Directions Partner".

Make a Connection

Step 1: Open the Contact List

  1. In Business Central, search for Contacts
  2. Open the Contacts list page

Step 2: Create a New Contact

  1. Click New to create a new contact
  2. Set the Company Name field to Directions Partner (exact spelling matters)
  3. Fill in any other required fields as needed
  4. Close the contact card

Why This Matters:

  • This task uses the event subscriber validation pattern
  • The task codeunit subscribes to OnAfterInsertEvent and OnAfterModifyEvent on the Contact table
  • The moment a Contact with Company Name "Directions Partner" is inserted or modified, the subscriber calls SetStatusCompleted()
  • The room status guard (GetStatus() <> InProgress) prevents the subscriber from firing outside this room
  • SingleInstance = true on the codeunit ensures the subscriber shares state with the task instance

Why both Insert and Modify? The participant might create the contact first (Insert) and then set the company name afterward (Modify). Subscribing to both events ensures the task completes regardless of the order.

Verification

  • A Contact with Company Name Directions Partner exists
  • Task completed automatically (no Update Status click needed)
  • Room 2 closes and Room 3 opens automatically

What You've Accomplished

  • Created contact data in Business Central
  • Experienced the event subscriber validation pattern — the task completed instantly without clicking Update Status
  • Understood why SingleInstance and room status guards are essential for event-based validation
